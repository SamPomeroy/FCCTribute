Anne Rice

Anne Rice (born Howard Allen Frances O'Brien) was an American author of gothic fiction, erotic literature and Christian literature. Rice published 37 novels and is best known for her Vampire Chronicles and Mayfair Witches series. These works explore the nature of good, evil and humanity through the lense of horror fiction. Also, under the psuedonym A.N. Roquelaure, Rice wrote The Sleeping Beauty Quartet. As well as publishing Exit to Eden and Belinda under the name Anne Rampling.
